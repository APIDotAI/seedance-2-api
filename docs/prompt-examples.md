# Prompt examples for Seedance 2

These prompts are written for testing the Seedance 2 API through APIDot. They are starting points for product teams, not guaranteed outputs. Adjust brand names, subject details, source URLs, and safety rules before production use.

## 1. Native audio product ad

A slow dolly-in on a ceramic cup of espresso in morning light, subtle steam, warm cafe ambience, synchronized room tone, cinematic product commercial.

## 2. Fast iteration clip

A handheld close-up of rain on a taxi window in neon city light, shallow depth of field, soft city ambience, short cinematic social clip.

## 3. Reference-driven scene

Use the reference image as the main character style, show the character walking through a quiet subway platform, consistent outfit, smooth tracking shot, natural footsteps.

## 4. First and last frame control

Animate from the first frame product close-up to the last frame wide hero shot, smooth camera pullback, consistent object shape, soft studio reflections.

## 5. Music-driven clip

A dancer moving through a minimal studio with rhythmic camera cuts, beat-synced motion, natural floor reflections, energetic but controlled choreography.

## 6. Storyboard preview

A two-shot cinematic sequence: first a close-up of a glowing map, then a wide shot of two explorers entering a neon train station, consistent characters and mood.

## Production prompt notes

- Store prompt versions together with task_id, model, user/job owner, and request payload.
- Keep source media URLs reachable long enough for APIDot to fetch them.
- Be explicit about camera motion, subject continuity, scene pacing, audio intent, duration, resolution, and aspect ratio.
- Do not log prompts or source media URLs if they may contain private customer data.
